About Us

We believe in raising a generation of men of virtue

Info

The Son initiative is a christian non-government organization dedicated to raising sons. Through a holistic approach, The son initiative addresses the spiritual,economic,social and physical aspects of the neediest sons within our reach in communities to help them become responsible and fulfilled virtuous men.

In 3 years of the son initiative intervention,over 1,000 teenage boys have shared the anger, sadness, and confusion they feel from growing up without a model man in their life.

Mission Statement

We care to advocate for sons, to release them from their spiritual, economic,social and physical fatherless needs and equip them to become responsible and fulfilled virtuous men. The empowered young men seize the opportunity to develop their full penitential. This entire purpose is embodied in the phrase: “Raising sons from fatherless needs in Jesus’ name”
